About MetroBreathe

MetroBreathe strives to provide an informative website designed to enhance people's understanding of the significance of the Air Quality Index (AQI).

Understanding The Urgency

In 2022, the Philippines faced challenges in air quality, ranking 69th among the world's most polluted regions. The PM2.5 levels, a measure of tiny particles harmful to health, were three times higher than the safety standards set by the World Health Organization. Although there was a temporary improvement during the 2020 lockdowns, pollution increased again as restrictions eased, reaching 14.9 μg/m³. (a unit that measures particulate matter in the air)

MetroBreathe

MetroBreathe is a smart air pollution monitoring system and smog prediction model developed for Metro Manila. It aims to provide real-time air quality data and predict smog occurrences, aiding local authorities in effective air quality management and promoting public health and environmental sustainability.
