Understanding The Urgency

In 2022, the Philippines ranked 69th among the world's most polluted regions, with PM2.5 levels three times higher than WHO safety standards. While there was a temporary improvement during the 2020 lockdowns, pollution rebounded as restrictions eased, reaching 14.9 μg/m³.
